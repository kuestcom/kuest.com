## What's actually under the hood

The contracts are derived from Polymarket's CLOB architecture, audited by
OpenZeppelin, and adapted for shared liquidity across multiple operator
frontends. Settlement uses UMA-based resolution rails. The matching engine
runs on Polygon mainnet — the same stack that's processed billions in
verified volume.

## How a typical institutional deploy works

Three steps. Days, not quarters.

1. **Define your market scope and fee model.** Pick the event categories
   that matter to your audience — macro, rates, politics, crypto, sports.
   Set your trading fee. Configure the brand surface.
2. **We deploy the full technical stack.** Smart contracts, CLOB, settlement
   rails, wallet infrastructure, liquidity — all running before your team
   finishes onboarding.
3. **Your platform earns a fee on every trade.** No revenue share, no
   intermediary, no settlement lag.
