Most prediction-market launches die quietly in their first month — not from a
bad product, but from an empty order book. New venues need depth before they
can attract traders, and they need traders before they can build depth. That
loop is brutal.

Shared liquidity solves the cold-start problem by routing real Polymarket
order flow through every market that mirrors a Polymarket question. The

first thousand traders at 4–6× the conversion rate of a cold-start venue,
because the perceived risk to the trader is much lower.
